## 学术界·前沿 (Academic)

### 🔥 [DynVLA: Learning World Dynamics for Action Reasoning in Autonomous Driving](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.11041) - 2026-03-11
- **核心创新**：提出了Dynamics CoT新范式，通过预测紧凑的世界动态来指导动作生成，实现更知情和物理基础的决策
- **应用潜力**：高
- **作者**：Shuyao Shang, Bing Zhan, Yunfei Yan, Yuqi Wang, Yingyan Li, Yasong An, Xiaoman Wang, Jierui Liu, Lu Hou, Lue Fan, Zhaoxiang Zhang, Tieniu Tan
- **代码状态**：代码已开源

### 🔥 [STADA: Specification-based Testing for Autonomous Driving Agents](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10940) - 2026-03-11
- **核心创新**：提出了基于形式化规范的自动驾驶智能体测试框架，系统生成由时态逻辑定义的所有可能场景
- **应用潜力**：中
- **作者**：Joy Saha, Trey Woodlief, Sebastian Elbaum, Matthew B. Dwyer
- **代码状态**：代码已开源

### 🔥 [Evaluating randomized smoothing as a defense against adversarial attacks in trajectory prediction](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.10821) - 2026-03-11
- **核心创新**：首次将随机平滑应用于轨迹预测模型的防御机制，提高对抗攻击下的鲁棒性
- **应用潜力**：中
- **作者**：Julian F. Schumann, Eduardo Figueiredo, Frederik Baymler Mathiesen, Luca Laurenti, Jens Kober, Arkady Zgonnikov
- **代码状态**：代码已开源
